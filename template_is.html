Wiki Icelandic HTR Project

Project description: the goal of this project is to collect a dataset of handwritten transcriptions of random fragments from Wikipedia. These texts will be used to train a handwriting recognition model.

If you would like your name to appear in the list of project participants, you may fill out the table below, though this is optional. The image of your transcription will be published online, but your name will be hidden in any case. All contributors who provide their names will be acknowledged in the credits.

Project contributors

This project was carried out by a group of researchers — Eli Magnanti, Svetlana Yatsyk, Sophia Chapple, María Florencia Vieira Ferrero, Maria Filimonova, Barbora Lunakova, and Arianna Locoteta — as part of the Antidote training week. It is inspired by the initiative of Alix Chagué (INRIA) and Thibault Clérice (École nationale des chartes), creators of the French WikiCremma project.
